Project Idea:weight loss insurance
Overview
Weight loss insurance will do its best to support everyone who wants to lose weight to achieve their dream body. To download the app, you need to pay 398aud in advance for insurance, a scale, a smartwatch, and a customized weight loss plan. Weight-loss insurance can make people more determined to lose weight. The weight-loss insurance will read users' real-time weight change, body fat rate change through the scale every day. After reaching the weight-loss target, the weight-loss insurance will refund the full amount and pay an extra 1000aud as a reward.
Motivation
According to statistics, in 2021, nearly 35% of Australian adults are overweight, and more than 30% are obese, which is quite a scary number. Because of the closure of gyms during the COVID-19 lockdown, coupled with snacking and home inactivity, more and more people are ignoring the potential problems of obesity. It is important for every Australian to be aware of the dangers of obesity and overweight. Weight loss insurance can provide targets for obese or overweight Australians, thus solving the problem of the high proportion of obese Australians.
Description
The specific service of weight loss insurance is divided into the following points:
1. Customize a personal weight-loss plan for users according to a series of information such as for overweight or obesity, gender, and how much time they have for losing weight every day. The plan that reduces weight insurance wants to accomplish reasonably, want the person that reduces weight to pay effort to be able to achieve a goal certainly only. Flexible, for different parts of the fat, has different action recommendations. Nutrition, weight loss is not by diet, but recommended to users’ low calorie but nutritious food, while avoiding any allergies.
2. Weight loss insurance is not only an insurance software but more like a forum for dieters. Dieters will share their weight loss experience in the forum for everyone to exchange. In addition, there are many professional weight-loss coaches and fitness coaches who often post in weight-loss insurance, or help users answer questions in real-time, and so on. At the same time, users will have a free one-on-one video class with a weight-loss coach once a week, during which the coach will help and coach users whether the exercise they do is standard.
3. Weight loss insurance will pass the scale real-time observed dieters weight changes, the user needs to wear in daily life with smartwatches, its function is to detect the user daily movement trajectory and AI algorithm is used to determine the user fairness and healthy to lose fat, once found that users take illegal way to lose weight like liposuction or a false behavior such as insurance fraud, Weight-loss insurance will ban its account. Weight-loss insurance offers rewards, but the real focus is on maintaining a healthy body.
4. The time limits are given to people who are overweight or obese vary, from six months for mildly obese people to more than a year for moderately obese people to more than two years for severely obese people. Insurance reducing weight believes fat is not temporary form, so reducing weight needs to rely on reasonable, safe, long-term means to undertake, cannot impatient, otherwise will be counterproductive.
5. Weight Loss Insurance has long-term cooperation with most gyms and fitness centers in Australia. Users can search nearby fitness places in the weight loss insurance app to quickly find fitness centers cooperating with weight loss insurance and enter them freely for free.
6. Users who achieve the goal of losing weight within the prescribed time can apply for compensation to the weight loss insurance, but it should be noted that within one month after the application of the claim, users still need to upload their weight and body fat percentage daily, to ensure that they are keeping a good body after achieving the goal. At the end of the month, the user can receive a refund of the insurance amount and receive an additional bonus.
Tools and Technologies
Weight loss insurance is considered to adopt HTML + Native hybrid technology for development. During the development process, complete requirements document such as flow charts, sequence diagrams, and preparation will be less problems for subsequent development. At the same time, we also need to conduct interaction design and UI design. After designing the basic APP interaction diagram and prototype diagram, we will adjust the overall UI interface according to this part of the content. We need to use Axure, Photoshop, Illustrator, and other software. We also need to use Java language to develop the app, because weight loss insurance needs to use the server, we need Web services development languages such as PHP and JSP.
Skills Required
1. We need to master advanced IT skills, such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other front-end development abilities, and constantly debugging to achieve a satisfactory effect, so we also need to be proficient in Axure and other interactive tools or Photoshop using.
2. Cross-platform capability should be satisfied, and cross-platform use should be achieved in IOS, Android, and other systems.
3. We need JAVA for Android-based development or Objective-C for iOS-based development for back-end development.
Outcome
If the project is successful, the results will affect the health level of the entire Australian people. First, those who have been obese or overweight, will not be worried about potential diseases due to obesity after using weight-loss insurance and will have a healthier body. For the whole of Australia, the ambiance of fitness will drive many people to join the active action of fitness. The Australian government will no longer need to worry about the high number of obese or overweight people, and the image of Australians in the international community will be further improved.
